Tteokbokki

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Noodles
  • Sugar

Cooking Instruction

  1. First, wash your hands
  2. Add water, salt and sugar to the flour
  3. Make little tteokbokki
  4. Boil them with Noodles
